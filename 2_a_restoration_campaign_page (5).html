The Issue

Millions of Americans who have served their time are still being punished—stripped of their Second Amendment rights for life. Most are nonviolent, reformed, and committed to rebuilding their lives.

  • 19+ million Americans are barred from owning firearms due to felony records
  • There is no automatic process for rights restoration—even after rehabilitation
  • This ban often leaves returning citizens more vulnerable, not safer

Our Solution

We created the Second Amendment Restoration and Reentry Justice Act to:

  • Restore rights 5 years after sentence completion for nonviolent felons
  • Allow individuals to petition for rights with due process
  • Punish misuse, not ownership
  • Fund public education and fair review systems
